Japanfes Volunteering

A volunteering oppurtunity for students who would also like to experience Japanese culture through a fun festival that is based in NYC.

For those who want to experience Japanese culture, Japanfes is the best event to attend if your in New York City. Of course it doesn't happen every day but it usually takes place during important japanese holidays. If you find the chance to attend it, you should! It was a great experience, both as a volunteer and a visitor.
