Infosys Challenge

The Infosys App Challenge is a challenge where mutiple teams from various schools first compete within their own school in the semi-finasl to get a chance at the finals where they would face off against the other schools for a grand price. Teams composes of mainly seniors along with a few juniors who work together to create a concept of a app that would address a problem in society that the students feel strongly about.

We, the Digital Engineers composed of me, Jordi Simbana, and Kaan Eren would come to a standing on the problem of stress amongst students, where we would mainly address the issue of time management within our age group and even those older and younger. We would use different coding languages to create a demostration of how the app would function and work together to create a slide that gave us the chance to become part of the finalist.
