CYOA Porject

A interactive adventure me and my partners Myron and Geo has worked on throughout the end of the first semester and at the start of the second semester.

A choose your own adventure centered around your start to the world of anime. You are given the options of 3 major genres in the anime shpere and have to choose which anime you would like to get yourself into first. Each genre presented you with at least 2 options to choose from and always presented a photo to get you interested.

Some problems we have ran into while working on this project was setting up some internal link jumps between .md's but we soon had it under controlled. Other issues were having internal link jumps break when it is within a line of a image, to get around it we just spaced them out to prevent further issues of such.
