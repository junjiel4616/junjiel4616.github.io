Freedom Project

Our (Me and my partner Jiang Hui Zheng) "Freedom Project" for sep11 used javascript and tools such as kaboomjs a javascript game programming library and firebase a set of backend cloud computing services and application development platforms to create a typing game that would keep track of your highest score.

Challenges

Challenges me and my partner encounter was mainly from that of learning our own tools throughout year as attempting to learn the tools on our own was by far more challenging then in a classroom enviorment. However, the most challenging challenge we encounter was not what we expected, the most challenging challenge that we ecountered was trying to combine both kaboomjs and firebase together by taking the score value of kaboomjs and giving it to firebase, something so simple sounding would become the most difficult part of our game.

Takeaway

  • Collaboration as me and my partner mainly worked seperated until the end as we thought it was quite simple
  • Making effective use of time as a lot more time could've been put into working things out and just learning and expermienting
  • Using help when necessary as figuring things out yourself can get confusing
